    TCP/IP stands for Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol. It is the fundamental communication protocol of the internet and is responsible for enabling communication between devices on a network. TCP/IP provides a set of rules and standards for how data is transmitted, routed, and received across the internet. It allows different types of devices and networks to communicate with each other, making it possible for users to access websites, send emails, and perform various online activities.
