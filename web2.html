    Web2 stands for the second generation of the World Wide Web. It is characterized by user-generated content, social media, and web applications that allow users to interact and collaborate with each other. Web2 introduced features such as blogs, wikis, social networking sites, and multimedia sharing platforms. It shifted the focus from static web pages to dynamic and interactive content, enabling users to create and share their own content online. Web2 has had a significant impact on how people communicate, share information, and engage with the internet.
