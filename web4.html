    Web4 stands for the fourth generation of the World Wide Web. It is characterized by artificial intelligence and machine learning integration into web technologies. Web4 aims to create a more intelligent and personalized web experience by leveraging AI algorithms to understand user preferences, automate tasks, and provide personalized content and recommendations. It focuses on enhancing user interactions, improving data analysis and decision-making, and enabling new forms of online experiences. Web4 is still in its early stages of development, but it has the potential to revolutionize how we use and interact with the internet in the future.
